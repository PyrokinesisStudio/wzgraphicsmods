Changes of NTW Mod Version 1.9.1:

*Note: This list is made for 1.10. I try to keep the Mod on both Warzone 2100 Versions

the same but it never will be 100%. So you may find things here which in 2.x not exist.

- Why NTW?  N = New, T = Team & W = War. In the old Warzone days was 1 Map very popular, Team War (TW). Today we play New Team War (NTW). This Map is inside the NTW Mod + my Map NTW ver2. -

- New look and a better AI (Computer Player). It may look differend in Software Mode. -

- Tiger class (faster, a bit more armor & less costs too) & Dragon are faster but a bit slower than Python. Wyvern is a bit faster than Python (btw. Python class got a bit more armor too and check the new Body classes out ^_^). VTOL Weapons are not so strong (bombs are a bit better than before) and Cyborgs are stronger than before (Weapons and Armor). Combat Engineer & Cyborg Mechanic do there job better too now. -

- Mini Rocket Artillery got the same range like Mortar. Tank Killer and Twin Assault Cannon got a range like Rail Gun -

- Ripple Rockets shoot 8x, Archangle Missile shoot 6x. The reload times of them are longer and they got better Names. Artillery Sites looks like a Site now. -

- Replaced Angel Missile in Condor Missile. It got Hellstorms range with much Damage and reload Time is 15-40 sec. The costs are high and it is a Artillery. Its on Archangle Missles place and it comes after Condor Missile. -

- Added a Mini Nuclear Missile Silo (Comes after Laser Sat and is stronger + more realod too). And there is a Nuclear Reactor with Cooling Towers which give you more Power. But only if you connect a Nuclear Reactor with 2 Cooling Towers. You need Vapor Turbine Generator Mk3 for it. -

- VTOL Truck, VTOL Sensor, VTOL CB Sensor, Bunker Buster Tower, Anti Aircraft Shield (Need VTOL Prop. & Neural Synapse Research Brain Mk1) and Cyborg Transporter with Heavy Pulse Laser (good  vs Buildings) are available -

- You need for Command Center: Sensor Turret & Sensor Tower. The other things are here: NTW Mod Research Tree

- Mobile Wide Spectrum Sensor is off and i didnt changed the info Text for the new Weapons... sorry. But there is a new Plasma Sniper Cyborg : ). You need Plasma Cannon for him. -

- Heavy Pulse Laser and Plasma Cannon are useful now (longer range, more damage, good vs Buildings). -

- Replaced AA Laser in Hyper Fire Laser (Hyper Fire Laser Defends got the same range like Scourge Missile Defends good vs Tanks and bad vs Buildings). It is better than Railgun and almost good like Gauss. -

- Replaced Flash Light Laser in Light Pulse Laser (short range) and Pulse Laser in Twin Pulse Laser (long range, good vs Buildings and bad vs Tanks). -

- Replaced HPV Cannon in Twin Heavy Cannon (good vs Tanks and needed for Gauss class). Its not like this one in 1.11 or 1.12 Patch! You need Heavy Cannon, HVAPFSDS Cannon Rounds Mk2 and Cannon Autoloader Mk1 for it. -

- Added Super Twin Cannon (a good anti Tank Weapon again). Its a bit better than Rail Gun but cost more. You need Twin Heavy Cannon and Cannon Rapid Loader Mk3 for it. -

- Another new Weapon is the Heavy Gauss Cannon. Its Heavyer than Gauss, got a slower reload, cost more and you need more time to produce it. But it do more Damage and got more Durability. Research Gauss Cannon for it. -

- Nexus Link got mor range, shoot during moving and you can build it faster with lower costs. Plasma Cannon Emplacement got the right Research Menu Icon now and Scorpion Body (VTOL and Wheels Bug) is repaired. -

- Flamers shoot during moving, got a bit longer range, are weaker with slower reload time and cost a bit more. good vs: soft Buildings, Bunker, Wheels, Hovers and bad vs: hard Buildings, half Tracks and Tracks -

- some Scavenger Buildings, -Units, Mines, Mines Sign, Cyborg Mine Sweeper and 2 New Body class are available. Btw. Lancer & Scourge reload speed Upgrade dont work (1.10 bug). Upgrade for Lancer is out and for Scourge used to Upgrade Condor Missile. -

- Assault Cannon (from 7500 to 1000) (You need just Medium Cannon for it), Twin Assault Cannon (from 8500 to 3000), Heavy Pulse Laser (from 20000 to 4000), Plasma Cannon (from 30000 to 6000), EMP Cannon (from 10000 to 5000), Heavy Cannon (from 10000 to 8000) and Medium Cannon (from 5000 to 4000) are not so heavy. -

- All Emplacements are Bunkers (costs rebalanced) and you can build all Fortresses faster with lower costs. Lancer Bunker cost 25 more now. -

- Replaced Sam Site class with Anti Ground Shield, Anti Aircraft Shield (both need Neural Synapse Research Brain) and Plasma Artillery with Groundshaker range (need Heavy Laser). -

- For EMP Stuff: Neural Synapse Research Brain Mk1 > EMP Cannon > Supercrete Mk1 > EMP Tank Traps. And you need the lower Class from the Hardponits and Bunkers, Ex.: Lancer Hardpoint > Tank Killer Hardpoint > Scourge Hardpoint. -
